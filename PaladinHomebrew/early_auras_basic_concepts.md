## The 12 Basic Auras

### 1. **Aura of the Hearth** (Community, Home, Protection)
*Associated Deity: **Boldrei** (Eberron) - Goddess of Community and Home*

**Theme:** The warmth of home and community surrounds allies, making them feel safe and supported. Boldrei's blessing strengthens the bonds between companions and bolsters their resolve.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against being frightened (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can use your Charisma modifier (minimum +1) for initiative rolls
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on saving throws against being frightened
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Charisma (Persuasion) checks when working together (once per turn)
- When an ally within 10 feet drops to 0 HP, they can make a death saving throw with a bonus equal to your CHA mod

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA (1d2 → 1d4 → 1d4+1d2 → advantage → max 2d4)

---

### 2. **Aura of the Forge** (Craftsmanship, Creation, Endurance)
*Associated Deity: **Moradin** (Forgotten Realms/Greyhawk) - God of Creation and the Forge*

**Theme:** The divine spark of creation strengthens allies' resolve and focus. Moradin's blessing grants allies the endurance of the forge, allowing them to maintain their concentration and perform precise actions.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Constitution saving throws (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on checks to maintain concentration
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to ability checks related to crafting, repair, or using tools (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can add your CHA mod to damage rolls with weapons (once per turn)

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA (1d2 → 1d4 → 1d4+1d2 → advantage → max 2d4)

---

### 3. **Aura of the Storm** (Tempest, Chaos, Power)
*Associated Deity: **Talos** (Forgotten Realms) or **Kord** (Greyhawk) - Gods of Storms and Strength*

**Theme:** The crackling energy of storms empowers allies and disrupts enemies. The raw power of the tempest flows through allies, while enemies find their focus disrupted.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Enemies within 10 feet have disadvantage on concentration checks
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Dexterity saving throws (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on Dexterity saving throws
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Strength (Athletics) checks (once per turn)

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### 4. **Aura of the Harvest** (Life, Growth, Sustenance)
*Associated Deity: **Chauntea** (Forgotten Realms) - Goddess of Agriculture and Life*

**Theme:** The bounty of the harvest nourishes and strengthens allies. Chauntea's blessing provides endurance and resilience, ensuring allies can endure long trials.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Constitution saving throws (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on Constitution saving throws
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Wisdom (Survival) checks (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can add your CHA mod to healing received (once per turn)

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### 5. **Aura of the Hunt** (Nature, Tracking, Instinct)
*Associated Deity: **Mielikki** (Forgotten Realms) or **Ehlonna** (Greyhawk) - Goddesses of Nature and the Hunt*

**Theme:** The keen senses of the hunter guide and protect allies. The goddess of the forest grants allies the sharp instincts of the wild, allowing them to track and strike with precision.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on Wisdom (Survival) and Wisdom (Perception) checks
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to attack rolls against creatures they've damaged this combat
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on initiative rolls
- Allies within 10 feet can't be surprised

**Scaling:** Attack bonus scales with CHA

---

### 6. **Aura of the Grave** (Death, Transition, Protection)
*Associated Deity: **Kelemvor** (Forgotten Realms) - God of Death and the Dead*

**Theme:** The boundary between life and death protects allies from premature endings. Kelemvor's judgment ensures that death comes only when it is truly time, protecting allies from untimely ends.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to death saving throws
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on death saving throws
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against necrotic damage (once per turn)
- Undead within 10 feet have disadvantage on attack rolls against your allies

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### 7. **Aura of the Light** (Truth, Revelation, Purity)
*Associated Deity: **Lathander** (Forgotten Realms) - God of Dawn and Renewal*

**Theme:** Divine radiance reveals truth and burns away deception. Lathander's morning light illuminates all, revealing hidden threats and purifying the hearts of allies.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can see invisible creatures within the aura
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against being charmed or frightened (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Wisdom (Perception) checks to detect illusions or hidden creatures (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet shed bright light in a 10-foot radius

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### 8. **Aura of the War** (Battle, Strategy, Courage)
*Associated Deity: **Tempus** (Forgotten Realms) or **Heironeous** (Greyhawk) - Gods of War and Valor*

**Theme:** The fervor of battle empowers allies and strikes fear into enemies. The war god's blessing fills allies with courage and martial prowess, while enemies feel the weight of impending defeat.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to attack rolls (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against being frightened (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on saving throws against being frightened
- Enemies within 10 feet have disadvantage on saving throws against being frightened
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Intelligence (History) checks related to warfare (once per turn)

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### 9. **Aura of the Knowledge** (Wisdom, Learning, Insight)
*Associated Deity: **Oghma** (Forgotten Realms) - God of Knowledge and Bards*

**Theme:** Divine wisdom guides allies' actions and reveals weaknesses. Oghma's blessing grants insight into all things, allowing allies to see through deception and understand their enemies' vulnerabilities.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to ability checks (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on Intelligence and Wisdom saving throws
- Allies within 10 feet can add your CHA mod to their passive Perception and Investigation
- When an ally within 10 feet makes an attack, they learn one of the target's damage resistances or vulnerabilities

**Scaling:** Bonus scales with CHA

---

### 10. **Aura of the Trickster** (Cunning, Luck, Misdirection)
*Associated Deity: **Tymora** (Forgotten Realms) - Goddess of Good Fortune and Luck*

**Theme:** Fortune favors the bold, and misfortune plagues enemies. Tymora's blessing turns the tides of fate, granting allies favorable outcomes while cursing enemies with ill luck.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can reroll 1d2 (scales) ability checks or saving throws per short rest
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Dexterity saving throws (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on Dexterity saving throws
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) and Charisma (Deception) checks (once per turn)
- Enemies within 10 feet must reroll attack rolls that are natural 20s (once per enemy per turn)

**Scaling:** Test bonuses and reroll amount scale with CHA

---

### 11. **Aura of the Sea** (Travel, Change, Depth)
*Associated Deity: **Valkur** (Forgotten Realms) - God of Sailors and Ships, or **Procan** (Greyhawk) - God of the Sea*

**Theme:** The ever-changing tides protect and guide allies. The sea god's blessing allows allies to adapt to any situation, moving with the fluidity of water and weathering any storm.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet have a swimming speed equal to their walking speed
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Wisdom (Survival) checks related to navigation or weather (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks to maintain balance (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can move through difficult terrain without penalty

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### 12. **Aura of the Oath** (Duty, Law, Commitment)
*Associated Deity: **Tyr** (Forgotten Realms) - God of Justice and Law*

**Theme:** The weight of sacred oaths protects those who keep their word. Tyr's blessing strengthens the resolve of those who honor their commitments, while those who break their word find their actions falter.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against being charmed or compelled (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on saving throws against being charmed
- Enemies within 10 feet who have broken oaths or deals have disadvantage on ability checks
- Allies within 10 feet can add your CHA mod to Charisma (Persuasion) and Charisma (Intimidation) checks

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA
