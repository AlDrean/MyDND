## Dragon Deities

### **Bahamut** (Lawful Good) - The Platinum Dragon, God of Justice and Protection
*Aura: **Aura of the Platinum Dragon** (Justice, Protection, Courage)*

**Theme:** Bahamut's noble presence instills courage and justice. His blessing protects allies from fear and draconic threats.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against being frightened (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on saving throws against being frightened
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against breath weapons (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet can add your CHA mod to Charisma (Persuasion) checks when seeking justice

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

---

### **Tiamat** (Lawful Evil) - The Chromatic Dragon, Goddess of Greed and Power
*Aura: **Aura of the Chromatic Dragon** (Dominance, Greed, Power)*

**Theme:** Tiamat's overwhelming presence dominates the battlefield. Her blessing empowers allies while weakening enemies' resolve.

**Effect Ideas:**
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to saving throws against being charmed (once per turn)
- Allies within 10 feet have advantage on saving throws against being charmed
- Enemies within 10 feet have disadvantage on saving throws against being frightened
- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Charisma (Intimidation) checks (once per turn)

**Scaling:** Test bonuses scale with CHA

- Allies within 10 feet can add 1d2 (scales) to Wisdom (Survival) checks in cold environments (once per turn)
